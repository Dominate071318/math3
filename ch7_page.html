Section 7-1

Zero Power & Negative Exponents

ANY number to the zero power equals 1. Negative exponents are NOT allowed — flip them to make them positive!

x⁰ = 1     x⁻ⁿ = 1/xⁿ (FLIP!)
  • 9⁰ = 1  |  122⁰ = 1  |  x⁰ = 1  |  −9⁰ = 1
  • 9⁻² → flip → 1/9² = 1/81
  • 1/4⁻³ → flip → 4³ = 64
  • −6² = −6·−6 = 36 (square first, then apply minus)
  • 5a³b⁻² → move b⁻² to bottom → 5a³/b²
  • Only move the term if its exponent is negative!
  • Calculator tip: put negative base in parentheses before raising to power
Section 7-3

Multiplying Powers (Same Base)

When multiplying terms with the SAME base, just ADD the exponents. If the coefficient is different, multiply the numbers AND add the exponents.

xᵃ · xᵇ = xᵃ⁺ᵇ  →  ADD exponents
  • 4⁸ · 4¹⁹ = 4²⁷  |  2³ · 2⁷ = 2¹⁰
  • 3x⁴ · 9x¹² = 27x¹⁶ (multiply 3×9, add 4+12)
  • 3x⁴ · 4x⁹ = 12x¹³
  • x⁻³ · 3x⁷ = 3x⁴ (add −3+7=4)
  • 2a · 9b⁴ · 3a² = 54a³b⁴
  • 2a³b² · 4ab⁻⁵ = 8a⁴b⁻³ → 8a⁴/b³ (flip the negative!)
Section 7-4

Power Raised to a Power

When you raise a power TO another power, MULTIPLY the exponents. This applies to EVERY term inside the parentheses!

(xᵃ)ᵇ = xᵃˣᵇ  →  MULTIPLY exponents
  • (x⁴)⁷ = x²⁸  |  (x⁻³)⁷ = x⁻²¹ → 1/x²¹
  • y³(y⁶)⁻² = y³ · y⁻¹² = y⁻⁷ = 1/y⁷
  • (4mn⁻²)³ = 4³m³n⁻⁶ = 64m³/n⁶
  • (3x²y⁴)³ = 3³x⁶y¹² = 27x⁶y¹²
  • (xy)⁰ = x⁰y⁰ = 1·1 = 1
  • Power applies to EVERY term inside parentheses — don't miss any!
Section 7-5

Dividing Powers (Same Base)

When dividing with the SAME base, SUBTRACT the exponents. The remainder goes where the BIGGER power was (top or bottom).

xᵃ/xᵇ = xᵃ⁻ᵇ  →  SUBTRACT (always largest − smallest)
  • x⁸/x³ = x⁵ (8−3=5, stays on top)
  • y²/y⁶ = 1/y⁴ (6−2=4, goes on bottom because 6 was bigger)
  • x¹⁴y²/x¹²y⁵ = x²/y³ (subtract each: 14−12=2 top, 5−2=3 bottom)
  • Power applies to everything inside parentheses: (x/y)⁵ = x⁵/y⁵
  • (3x²/y³)³ = 27x⁶/y⁹
  • Move negative exponents FIRST before solving
Chapter 7 Test Prep

Quick Reference — All Rules Together

Zero & Negative

  • x⁰ = 1
  • (3xy)⁰ = 1
  • x⁻² = 1/x²
  • (x²)⁻⁵ = 1/x¹⁰

Multiply & Power

  • y² · y⁴ = y⁶
  • 2x⁵ · 9x³ = 18x¹⁵ (note: 5+3=8 but wait! It's actually 18x⁸)
  • (x²)⁴ = x⁸
  • (x⁴y⁻²)³ = x¹²/y⁶

Divide

  • y⁵/y³ = y²
  • y³/y⁵ = 1/y²
  • (xy)⁻²(xy)³ = xy
  • Neg exp: FLIP first!
