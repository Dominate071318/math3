Quick Math Tips

🐊
Inequality Trick

The alligator always eats the BIGGER number. The open mouth (< or >) faces the bigger side.

🚂
Parallel Lines

Think train tracks — parallel lines have the SAME slope but different y-intercepts. They never cross!

📐
PEMDAS Order

Parentheses → Exponents → Multiply/Divide (left to right) → Add/Subtract (left to right)

⚡
Flip the Sign!

Dividing or multiplying by a NEGATIVE number in an inequality? Always flip the inequality sign!

🎯
Slope Formula

m = (y₂ − y₁) ÷ (x₂ − x₁). Rise over run. Only 1 negative sign in your answer = negative slope.

💡
Negative Exponents

Can't have negative exponents! FLIP the base from top to bottom (or bottom to top) to make it positive.
